2013-10-02 - International compliance regulations to get tougher: Banks are facing a rising legal requirement from governments to not only "know your customer" but also to know who is making payments to a customer and to whom the customer is making payments. As a result, banking software systems are being upgraded across the industry to require reporting to the bank of all incoming funds before they even arrive at the bank.

Our Deposit Notification allows you to comply with legal requirements in this respect. If someone wants to send funds to you, ensure that you have authorised it and completed a deposit notification defining who the payor is BEFORE the funds are sent to the bank.

If you do not do this, then no matter where you bank there is a high risk that the funds will be refused and returned from whence they came. This is one part of the international fight against money laundering of the proceeds of crime and the prevention of the funding of terrorism.
